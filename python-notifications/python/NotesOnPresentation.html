Notes from Senior Project Presentations

  • Make sure Powerpoint is easy to read in lighting, some templates are better than vanilla PowerPoint for this reason.

  • Possible Templates
    • Ion Template
    • Red Carpet

  • Dress similar color for team feeling, or atleast everyone dress professional.

  • Practice with detail; have diagrams and or demos to talk through process of user (recording of features for people to see while talking though details)
    • In Powerpoint: Click Insert, then click on videos on the top left of menu to add video from computer.

  • Make sure everyone talks, don't have fish eyes and be sure to have small smiles instead of deadpan expressions while teammates are talking.

  • Talk about the code (process, relation, and features), do focus on the code-side and the user interaction side.

  • It may be a good idea to practice in the CEB Classroom ahead of time to make sure configurations are working the way we want it for presentation.

  • Show database connections, on how the database connects with its tables.

  • Try to minimize the number of times you say "um", small pauses seem better than hearing "um" constantly.
